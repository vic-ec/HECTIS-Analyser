Help & User Guide
How to use HECTIS Analyser — Victoria Hospital Wynberg Emergency Centre

Getting Started

  • Go to Upload and drag your monthly Excel file onto the upload zone.
  • The app parses, validates, and inserts records into the HECTIS database automatically.
  • Duplicate records are silently skipped — safe to re-upload the same file.
  • To load historical data, upload multiple files at once.

Filters

The filter bar applies to all tabs simultaneously. Filter by date range, discipline, triage category, or trauma type. Click Reset to restore the full dataset.

Generating a Report

  • Click 🖨 Report in the filter bar at any time.
  • A management summary opens in a new tab with your current filtered data.
  • Click Print / Save PDF in that page to save or print.
  • The report includes KPIs, access block by discipline, triage compliance, and auto-generated interpretation.

Understanding the Tabs

  • Overview — Top-level KPIs and trend charts.
  • Access Block — Boarding times per discipline. Core management metric.
  • Time Patterns — Heatmap identifying when access block peaks.
  • Triage Compliance — Triage-to-doctor times vs SATS-based targets.
  • Trauma — Trauma vs non-trauma comparison and specific trauma types.
  • Data Table — Full row-level data. Searchable, sortable, exportable.

Key Metrics Explained

  • Access block — Disposal-to-exit time exceeding 4 hours. Reflects inpatient capacity failure.
  • Disposal → Exit — Decision to admit → patient physically leaving EC bed.
  • Total LOS — Arrival to exit. Dominated by boarding for admitted patients.
  • Triage compliance — % seen by EC doctor within target for their triage category.
  • P75 / P90 — 75th and 90th percentiles. More meaningful than maximums.
